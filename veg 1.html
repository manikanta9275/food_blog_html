Vegetarian Recipes


1.THAI CORN FRITTERS:

FAMOUS :Indonesia

INGREDIENTS:

● 500 gms Fresh or Frozen Corns
● 3 Spring Onions (Chopped)
● 1 large Red Chilli (Chopped)
● 1 tsp Grated Ginger
● 2 tbsp Coriander Leaves (Chopped)
● 4 tbsp Coconut Milk
● Oil To Fry 		● 1 tsp Sesame Oil
● to taste Black Salt
● 1 Egg
● 90 gram All Purpose Flour
● 1/2 tsp Baking Powder

Process:

1.Add flour, black salt and baking powder to a medium-sized mixing bowl. Gradually pour in coconut milk while whisking to make a smooth batter and then add the oil.
2.If you need to defrost the corn, boil water in a saucepan and add the corn. Let it boil for 4-5 minutes and then drain.
3.Mix the corn into the bowl containing the batter.
4.Add chopped spring onion, red chilli, grated ginger, coriander, lime juice, soy sauce, sesame seed oil, salt, egg, all-purpose flour, baking powder. Mix everything together.
5.Now, heat oil in a non-stick pan. Add a spoonful of corn batter to the pan to make the fritters. Fry for 3 to 4 minutes on each side. Remove the fritters from the pan and serve with some ketchup.



2.VEG MAKHANI

FAMOUS : India

INGREDIENTS:

● 100 Gram Cauliflower
● 100 gram Potatoes
● 8-10 pieces Beans
● 100 gram Carrots
● 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
● 1/2 tsp Red Chilli Powder
● 2 tbsp Cashew
● 1/2 inch Ginger
● 3-4 pieces Garlic
● 2 tbsp Butter 		● 1 Tej Patta
● 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
● 1 Green Chilli
● 1/3 cup Frozen Peas
● 200 gram Tomatoes
● 2-3 tbsp Cream
● 1/2 tsp Sugar
● to taste Salt
● 1.5 cup Water

Process:

1.Sautee the chopped cauliflower, potatoes, beans, carrots and peas. Keepthis aside.
2.Soak cashew in hot water for 30 mins. Grind the soaked cashew, chopped tomato, ginger and garlic and make a smooth paste.
3.Heat butter in a pan. Saute tej patta and the tomato-cashew paste. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and fry the spices. Pour the water and add the green chilli. Let it cook for a while.
4.Once it thickens and add the sauted vegetables, garam masala, sugar, salt and the cream. Combine this to the gravy and remove from flame.
5.Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve the veg makhani hot!



3.POTATO RINGS

FAMOUS :SOUTH KOREA

INGREDIENTS:

● 1 tsp Chilli flakes
● 1 tsp Oregano
● Salt as per taste
● 2 Boiled potatoes
● 1/4 cup Rava (Semolina)

Process:

1.In a pan add some butter, garlic, chilli flakes and oregano. Then once it is cooked add some water.
2.Let it boil and add rava.
3.Once the rava soaks the water, allow it to cool down.
4.Next, add two mashed potatoes to it and form a dough.
5.Flatten out this dough and cut out small rings from it using a cutter. Or, you can also cut long strips from it and then make a circle from it.
6.Sprinkle some cornflour and deep fry till crispy.



4.CABBAGE CUTLET

FAMOUS :FRANCE

INGREDIENTS:

● 2 Cups Grated cabbage
● 3 tbsp Chopped coriander
● 1 tsp Chopped garlic
● 1 Onion (Finely Chopped)
● 1 tsp Chaat masala
● 1 tsp Corn flour
● 3/4 tsp Red chilli powder 		● 3/4 tsp Cumin Seeds
● 1 tsp Salt
● 1 tsp Salt
● 1/4 tsp Pepper powder
● 1/4 tsp Pepper powder
● 1 tsp Corn flour
● 7 tbsp Sooji

Process:

1.Wash and strain the grated cabbage, add the chopped coriander, onion and garlic. Mix it well.
2.Next, add all the masalas – chaat masala, red chilli powder, cumin seeds, salt and pepper powder.
3.Add the corn flour and half of the sooji at first, mix it well. Then gradually add the rest. Combine the mixture well and let it rest for at least 10 minutes.
4.Make round-shaped cutlets and shallow fry them till both sides are golden brown.
5.The cabbage cutlet is ready!



5.CHILLI IDLE

FAMOUS :INDIA

INGREDIENTS:

● 6-7 Idli
● 1 large Onion (Cubed)
● 1 medium Capsicum
● 3-4 Green Chillies (Chopped)
● 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
● 1 tsp Garlic (Chopped)
● 1 tsp Black Pepper
● to taste Salt 		● 1 tbsp Red Chilli Sauce
● 1 tbsp Soya Sauce
● 1 tbsp Vinegar
● 2 tbsp Corn Starch
● 1 tbsp Maida
● Oil as required

Process:

1.You can use leftover idli or you can also use fresh idli to make chilli idli.
2.Take idlis and cut them into desired shape. Make a thin batter by mixing corn starch, all purpose flour, salt, pepper and ginger garlic paste in a bowl.
3.Heat oil in a pan, coat the idlis well in the batter and fry them in oil till they turn crispy golden.
4.After this, heat oil in another pan, add finely chopped garlic, green chili and onion and fry it lightly.
5.Add capsicum, black pepper and a little salt to it. Now add red chili sauce, ketchup, vinegar and soy sauce to it.
6.Cook all these things for few seconds. Add corn starch liquid to it and mix.
After this add fried idli and mix it well. Serve garnished with green onions.



6.SPINACH WRAP

FAMOUS :MEXICO

INGREDIENTS:

● To make Spinach Bread:
● 1 cup Spinach puree
● 2 cups wheat flour
● salt to taste
● 2 tsp oil

To prepare the filling:

● 250 g paneer
● 2 onions, sliced
● 1 medium bell pepper, sliced
● 1 medium tomato, cut into pieces
 ● Garlic
● 2 green chillies, finely chopped
● 1 tsp red pepper
● 1 tsp coriander powder
● salt to taste
● 2 tbsp oil
● juice of 1/2 lemon
● 2 tbsp green chutney
● 1 tbsp mayonnaise

Process:

1.Take wheat flour in a vessel, add salt, oil and spinach puree to it and knead the dough. Keep it aside for some time.
2.Now put a pan on the gas to make filling. Heat oil in it and add green chillies along with garlic and fry for a few seconds.
3.After this, cook onion, capsicum for a while. Then add tomatoes and fry again.
4.Add salt, red chili, coriander powder, mix everything and cook. Add paneer and mix well with the mixture. At last mix lemon juice and turn off the gas.
5.Heat the griddle on the gas and make dough balls. Roll it in the shape of a roti and roast it well on a griddle.

For Assembling:

1.Take spinach roti, spread this mayonnaise and spread it after adding the prepared filling. Add green chutney.
2.Roll the roti and wrap it in butter paper and serve. Your spinach wrap is ready.



7.BEETROOT PULAO

FAMOUS :INDIA

INGREDIENTS:

● 2 Onions, sliced
● 2 Tomatoes, chopped
● 2 Potatoes, chopped
● 1 small Beetroot, finely chopped
● 1 cup Rice
● 1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
● 1 Green chilli, slit 		● 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
● 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
● 1 tsp Coriander Powder
● 1 tsp Garam Masala
● Salt to taste
● Red chilli powder to taste

Process:

1.Soak rice in water for at least half an hour.
2.Fry cumin seeds in hot oil; saute onions, green chilli and ginger-garlic paste.
3.Saute tomatoes, salt and all the spices.
4.Add chopped beetroot and potatoes and rice.
5.Add water and let it all cook. Serve hot.



8.SCHEZWAN DOSA

FAMOUS :India

INGREDIENTS:

● 2 and a half Cup Dosa Batter
● 3/4 cup Noodles (Boiled)
● 1/4 cup Cabbage (Julienned)
● 1/4 cup Carrot (Julienned)
● 1/4 cup Capsicum (Julienned)
● 3 tbsp French Beans (Cut Diagonally)
● 1/2 tsp Ginger (Finely Chopped)
● 1 tsp Garlic (Finely Chopped)
● 2 Green Chillies (Cut Diagonally) 		● 3 tbsp Spring Onion Greens (Cut Lengthwise)
● 1 tsp Soy sauce
● 3/4 cup Schezwan Sauce
● 1 cup Rice Vinegar
● 2 tsp Tomato Ketchup
● 1/2 tsp Black Pepper Powder
● Salt to taste
● 3 tsp Vegetable (Plus for cooking dosa)

Process:

1.To prepare the filling for dosa, heat oil in a wok then add ginger and garlic when oil becomes hot. Saute for just few seconds.
2.Now, add onions, green chillies and saute on high heat for 30 secs. Add capsicum, carrots, cabbage and beans. Fry them on high heat for 2-3 mins.
3.Veggies should remain crunchy. Add boiled noodles along with 2 teaspoon Schezwan sauce, tomato sauce, soy sauce, vinegar, pepper and salt.
4.Toss them well to coat everything with sauces nicely. Switch off the flame and mix in spring onion greens. Let the stuffing cool down little bit.
5.Now, heat a non stick griddle pan. Brush lightly with oil. Wipe it with a tissue. Pour one ladleful of dosa batter and spread it to make big dosa.
6.Smear some oil on sides and top of the dosa. Now, spread the Schezwan sauce nicely on the top of dosa.
7.the dosa become nicely crisp from bottom and cooked from top as well. No need to flip and cook the other side.
8.Place some stuffing on one side of dosa and carefully lift the other side and fold into half moon. Take it out on to a plate.



9.BUTTER GARLIC MUSHROOMS

FAMOUS :CHINA

INGREDIENTS:

● 1 Chopped mushrooms
● 1 tbsp Garlic
● 1 tbsp Oregano
● 2 tbsp Butter 		● to taste Salt
● to taste Black Pepper

Process:

1.Wash the mushrooms under running water and boil them in water till they are soft.
2.Take a pan and add butter and garlic. Let the flavour of garlic get infused in butter.
3.Then add the mushrooms and throw in salt, pepper and oregano.
4.Cook it on a medium flame for 10 minutes.



10.PANEER PANCAKE

FAMOUS :SOUTHEAST EUROPE

INGREDIENTS:

● 250 gms Grated Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese)
● 2 Whole Eggs , or Flax Meal Egg Replacer
● 3 Tbsp Sugar
● 1/2 tsp Baking powder
● 1/2 Cup Whole Wheat Flour 		● Salt , a pinch
● 3 Tbsp Milk
● 2 Tbsp Butter (Salted)

Process:

1.Beat eggs in a clean and dry bowl till light and fluffy.
2.Add sugar and beat till the sugar is combined. Stir in the smooth paneer, baking powder, plain flour and salt to the beaten egg mixture. pancake batter.
3.Heat a skillet over low heat. Melt some butter and pour in a large ladle of the paneer pancake batter onto the pan. The batter will spread on its own and settle down.
4.When you notice bubbles appear on top, flip over and cook the paneer pancake the other side.
5.Once done, proceed the same way with the remaining batter and transfer the paneer pancakes to a serving plate.
6.Serve the Paneer Pancake topped with fruits and honey or maple syrup as a wholesome high protein breakfast.
